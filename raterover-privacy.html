Privacy Policy

RateRover

Effective Date: December 23, 2025

RateRover (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "this App") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy aims to clearly explain how we handle your information.

In short: We do not collect your personal data. All your input data is stored solely on your local device.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 User Input Data

This App does not upload the amounts, preferences, or conversion history you input to any of our servers. All such data is stored entirely locally on your device (Local Storage).

1.2 Currency Exchange Data Acquisition

To provide accurate currency conversion services, this App requires internet access to fetch the latest exchange rate data.

  • Data Source: Our exchange rate data comes directly from the public API of the European Central Bank (ECB).
  • Data Content: We only request historical and real-time exchange rate data; this process does not involve sending any of your personally identifiable information.

2. Use of Third-Party Services

To ensure the stability and functionality of the App, we integrate the following third-party services. These services may collect specific non-personally identifiable information (such as crash logs or network request statuses) in accordance with their privacy policies:

Google Firebase

We use Firebase to support the App's infrastructure. Firebase may collect certain technical data (e.g., device model, OS version, crash logs) to help us diagnose issues and optimize user experience. This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Read Google Privacy Policy →

European Central Bank (ECB) SDK/API

We use the ECB interface to synchronize exchange rate data. This interaction is limited to the retrieval of public data.

3. Data Storage and Security

Since we do not collect your personal data, we do not maintain any cloud databases for storing user personal information. Your data security primarily relies on the security mechanisms of your device itself. We recommend that you protect your device to prevent data leakage.

4. Permissions

To use RateRover normally, the App requires the following permission:

  • Internet Access: Used to connect to the European Central Bank servers to update the latest exchange rates.

5. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we do not collect any user data, this commitment naturally applies to users of all ages.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Since the App does not collect user contact information, we recommend that you periodically review this page for the latest information.
